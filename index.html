Information

Land Buoy is here a website to host the audio visual sketches made by Max Coker. The name comes from his time on the airwaves at WUSB Stony Brook 90.1FM. his practice explores the objectivity of digital sound and video by designing models and transferring to physical entities. landbuoy expressions are modeled in virtual space for interactive performance systems.
